The Bold Opinion

BUILD CREDIBILITY

Best for building credibility, sparking engagement, and showing your POV. Don't hedge—commit to the take.

Hook (0-5 Sec)

"Unpopular opinion: [common belief] is actually [why it's wrong]."

Body (5-45 Sec)

Here's what most people miss:

[Explain your contrarian take in 1-2 punchy sentences.]

I've seen [specific example]. And the result? [compelling outcome].

CTA

Do you agree? Drop a [emoji] below or fight me in the comments.
OPERATOR PRO TIP

Bold opinions get shares. The more provocative, the better the algorithm treats you.
